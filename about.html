Our Story

Paws & Bubbles began as a dream between lifelong dog lovers, Jessie and Taylor, who believed grooming should be a calming, positive experience. From a humble home setup to a full-service salon, our mission has remained the same: to treat every dog like family.

Our Philosophy

We use gentle techniques, high-quality products, and patient care. Our groomers are trained in dog behavior and grooming safety to ensure your furry friend not only looks great but feels great, too.
